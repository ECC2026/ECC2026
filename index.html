Welcome to ECC 2026

It is our greatest pleasure to welcome you to The 10th Euro-China Conference on Intelligent Data Analysis and Applications (ECC 2026), hosted by Wuxi University, Wuxi , China. The conference will be held from September 04, 2026 to September 06 , 2026 at the Wuxi University, No.333 Xishan Avenue, Wuxi, Jiangsu, China, Wuxi, China. Call for paper

The 10th Euro-China Conference on Intelligent Data Analysis and Applications (ECC 2026) will be hosted by Wuxi University , Wuxi, China, on September 05, 2026. The venue location is the Wuxi University, No.333 Xishan Avenue, in Wuxi, China. The conference provides a forum for researchers, engineers, and scientists to discuss information hiding, multimedia signal processing, the application of artificial intelligence in multi-media and signal processing, and information technology to exchange the latest research outcomes. Multimedia technologies facilitate the creation of global information infrastructure for acquiring, storing, and communicating data in different forms. The proliferation of multimedia applications raises challenges such as multimedia security and privacy, big data in multimedia, and intelligence in multimedia processing. This conference was established in Melbourne, Australia in 2005 and was subsequently held in USA, Taiwan, Germany, China, Greece, Australia, Vietnam, Korea, and Japan from 2006 to 2025.

About Wuxi


  Wuxi, known as the "Pearl of Taihu Lake", is a renowned national historical and cultural city with a history stretching back more than 3,000 years. As one of the birthplaces of Wu Culture, it has nurtured profound traditions, elegant aesthetics, and outstanding figures throughout history. Located in the prosperous Yangtze River Delta, Wuxi enjoys a superior geographical location, connecting the Yangtze River in the north and facing Taihu Lake in the south. It is a dynamic modern city that perfectly integrates profound cultural heritage, beautiful natural scenery, and advanced industrial development. Wuxi boasts world-famous scenic spots: the picturesque Yuantouzhu, the magnificent Lingshan Grand Buddha, the time-honored Huishan Ancient Town, and the charming Grand Canal Historic District. It is not only a famous land of fish and rice but also an important economic, technological and cultural city in eastern China. Today, Wuxi stands as a city of openness, vitality and charm, welcoming friends from all over the world.
